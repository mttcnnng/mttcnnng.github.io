Company

A London computing and digital services company focused on practical technology solutions for small and growing businesses.

Why it existed

It addressed demand for straightforward web, server and software support at a time when many businesses were moving online but lacked accessible technical delivery.

My role

Co-founded the company and helped define its cost-effective delivery model, services and customer proposition.

What we built

Services included web design, server installation, interactive website features, e-commerce solutions and bespoke software development.

How it was delivered

Delivery centred on practical consulting, hands-on implementation and custom technical work shaped around each client’s immediate business need.

Outcome

Opened and closed in 2000 after exploring practical digital delivery for companies that needed useful technology without unnecessary complexity.
