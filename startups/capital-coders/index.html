Capital Coders Limited

A UK software company that began with TraderAnalytics and grew into a hedge fund software consultancy.

Related build: TraderAnalytics.com

Company

A London software company serving hedge funds and financial technology teams with trading analytics and bespoke front-office tools.

Why it existed

It was created to turn specialist trading and portfolio-management software experience into reusable products and consultancy delivery for hedge fund clients.

My role

Founded the company, built and marketed TraderAnalytics, and led delivery of bespoke hedge fund software projects.

What we built

TraderAnalytics plus consultancy systems spanning trading performance analytics, portfolio risk tooling, Reuters-backed option chains, VBA add-ins and bespoke hedge fund applications.

How it was delivered

Work combined product development, client delivery and financial-domain engineering across C#, ASP.NET, SQL-backed analytics, reporting workflows and desktop or spreadsheet-based tools where client workflows required them.

Outcome

Grew from a startup product effort into a London hedge fund software consultancy between 2007 and 2009.
