Knowledge Matching App

A mobile application concept that transformed personal contact networks into a trusted knowledge marketplace.

Concept

A mobile application concept that transformed personal contact networks into a trusted knowledge marketplace.

Why it matters

The idea uses trusted relationships and referral incentives to help questions, requests and opportunities reach the right person.

How it could work

Users could post a request with an optional finder's fee, share it with selected contacts and reward the referral chain when a successful match was made.

Status

Mobile app concept conceived in 2016.
