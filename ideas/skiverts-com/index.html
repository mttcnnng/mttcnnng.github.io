A concept for turning ski tips into seasonal advertising space.

Why it matters

The idea gives advertisers repeated visibility in resort environments without changing the ski-hire workflow.

How it could work

Ski-hire companies and resorts could place branded stickers on rental skis for visibility on lifts, slopes and around resort areas.
