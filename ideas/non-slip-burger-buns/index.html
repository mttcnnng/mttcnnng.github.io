Non-Slip Burger Buns

A redesigned burger bun that helps prevent the patty and fillings from sliding out with every bite.

Concept

A redesigned burger bun that helps prevent the patty and fillings from sliding out with every bite.

Why it matters

It tackles a familiar eating problem without changing the taste or overall experience of a burger.

How it could work

The bun would use a shaped or textured internal surface to grip the contents more securely for restaurants, takeaways and home use.

Status

Product concept conceived in 2023.
