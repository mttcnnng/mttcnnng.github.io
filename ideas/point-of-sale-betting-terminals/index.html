Point-of-Sale Betting Terminals

An explored concept for self-service betting exchange terminals using touchscreen technology, telephone voice recognition and SMS.

Concept

An explored concept for self-service betting exchange terminals using touchscreen technology, telephone voice recognition and SMS.

Why it matters

The idea tested whether betting exchange access could move into public venues such as pubs.

How it could work

Terminals would let users interact through touchscreens, voice recognition or SMS in venue-based point-of-sale settings.

Status

Concept explored in 2003 and not pursued after direct discussions and strong advice from the Department for Culture, Media and Sport.
