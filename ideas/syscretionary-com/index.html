Concept

A past concept for a chart-based trading experience that blended discretionary market judgement with rules-based strategy discipline.

Why it matters

It explored the space between instinct and system, giving traders a way to decide when and where a predefined strategy made sense.

How it could work

The experience imagined traders spotting an opportunity on a chart, choosing a strategy and applying it to that moment while preserving a consistent approach.
