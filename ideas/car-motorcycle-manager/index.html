Car & Motorcycle Manager

A Visual Basic application for managing maintenance records across a personal collection of cars and motorcycles.

Concept

A Visual Basic application for managing maintenance records across a personal collection of cars and motorcycles.

Why it matters

The idea gave vehicle owners a central record of service history, repairs and upcoming maintenance tasks.

How it could work

Users could track completed and upcoming service tasks, organise receipts and keep a history for each vehicle.

Status

Developed in Visual Basic in 1996.
