Postbox

A past concept for turning chosen digital updates into physical morning printouts.

Concept

A past concept for a home printer that turned chosen digital updates into physical morning printouts.

Why it matters

It aimed to make personalised updates feel as effortless as picking up the post, especially for commuters and households with daily routines.

How it could work

The experience imagined people subscribing to the updates they cared about and receiving a compact printed briefing without needing to browse multiple services.
