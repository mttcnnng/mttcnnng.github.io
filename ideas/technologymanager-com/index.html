Concept

A past concept for helping organisations understand what technology they owned, who was responsible for it and what it cost to run.

Why it matters

It addressed the management fog that appears when assets, suppliers, renewals, costs and ownership are scattered across teams.

How it could work

The experience imagined a shared management view where leaders could see important assets, responsibilities, spend and upcoming decisions in one place.
